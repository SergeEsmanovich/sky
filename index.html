Skywatch is a comprehensive monitoring and alerting platform for all SAP environments. It contains hundreds of highly customizable exception indicators, categorized and organized into packages. Each package covers specific area of usage, allowing controlling the following aspects of SAP operations.

Skywatch helps users, managers and technical teams to optimize accelerate and facilitate their day by day SAP routines. With Skywatch you can notify anybody about any SAP event, via email or text message. Using Skywatch Online Monitor, it is easy to track and observe all events at a single screen, jumping to the problem with just one click. Skywatch can be installed on any SAP system in minutes and become fully operational in a matter of hours – no additional hardware or software is required.

Between the years 2006 - 2010, according to the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners
